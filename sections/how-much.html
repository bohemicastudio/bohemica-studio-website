Hourly-based

Our favourite pricing method is using an hourly-based pricing with a minimum and maximum time range for each milestone.

  • It's difficult to know how much time each task will actually take due to unforeseen circumstances or other project nuances
    • Fixed amount might not accurately represent the value/work/time
    • Hourly rate has usually no boundaries, and therefore may go outside client's budget
  • Time range caps the maximum so client knows exactly what the highest cost might be
  • It also gives our team more breathing room if any task takes take longer than anticipated
